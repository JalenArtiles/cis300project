Plan better trips with less stress

Roam Ready is a student-created travel guidance website designed to help visitors feel more confident before booking a trip. The website gives useful information about destinations, travel organization, budgeting, and where to find reliable travel resources.

The goal is not to overwhelm users with too much information. Instead, each page is focused, readable, and built to help travelers make smart first decisions.
